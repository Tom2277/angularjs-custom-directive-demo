No Angular on this Page... and no use of angular-ui-router

For static pages you'll need to cut and paste the Navbar HTML using 'href's in place of the 'ui-sref's that you use to access view template 'pages' on a page managed by the angular-ui-router.

Of course, Cut and pasting leads to errors and inconsistency (which I have purposefully demonstrated!). Reverting to separate pages from consistently generated pages means more repetitive work for each change you make.

These freestanding pages will also require full HTML boilerplate including references to all the JS and Stylesheet documents.
